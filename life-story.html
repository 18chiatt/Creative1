life story

When I was 10, my parents decided to have us move to China. My dad works in the board Game publishing space and he wanted to be closer to the manufacturing so we could get cheaper prices. At first none of us spoke the language and getting around was difficult. For the first two years, we went to a lame school where we learned basically nothing. After that, we moved to a school that worked better for us, which was great.

After the first couple years, my siblings and I became pretty proficient in the language and started meeting a lot of people and making a lot of friends
